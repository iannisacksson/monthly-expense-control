# Product Vision — Personal Monthly Financial Manager

## Context

Many people struggle to maintain clear visibility over their personal financial situation.
Common problems include:

* Lack of structured tracking of monthly expenses
* Difficulty understanding where money is spent
* No clear planning for monthly budgets
* Poor visibility of recurring obligations and installment commitments
* Lack of tools to compare planned vs actual spending

Most existing financial tools are either:

* too generic for month-centered personal planning
* overly complex financial systems
* or weak in budgeting, recurring expenses, and installment visibility

Additionally, users with multiple sources of income (salary, freelance, business, bonus) need flexibility to track variable monthly income.

The product aims to provide a simple but structured system to help each user manage monthly finances with clarity.

---

## Vision

Create a financial management application that allows users to:

* Track monthly income for each month
* Categorize and monitor expenses
* Plan budgets based on customizable financial rules (e.g., 50/30/20)
* Monitor spending by category
* Control recurring and installment expenses with clear monthly visibility
* Access category management directly from the monthly flow without hidden actions
* Compare planned vs actual spending using net income after taxes
* Understand their financial position through clear dashboards

The system should provide clarity, simplicity, and control in personal monthly financial management.

---

## Target Users

Primary users:

* Individuals managing their own monthly finances
* Professionals with fixed and variable income
* Users who need stronger monthly control over recurring and installment expenses

Secondary users:

* Freelancers with variable monthly income
* Self-employed users managing irregular monthly cash flow

---

## Core Product Principles

### Simplicity First

The system must remain easy to use.
Complex financial concepts should be simplified for everyday users.

---

### Monthly Financial Structure

The application is structured around **monthly financial cycles**, allowing users to:

* define income per month
* track expenses per month
* compare planned vs actual spending

---

### Personal Ownership

Each user owns their own financial workspace.

All primary financial operations occur inside the context:

User + Month

The product must not depend on family membership or shared ownership as the main product flow.

---

### Financial Transparency

The system should help users clearly understand:

* where money is going
* how spending compares to the budget
* which categories exceed limits

---

### Flexible Financial Rules

The system supports customizable budgeting rules, such as:

* the traditional **50 / 30 / 20 rule**
* fully customized allocation strategies

---

## Core Capabilities

The MVP will support:

1. Month creation and monthly navigation per user
2. Monthly income tracking per user
3. Expense tracking with categories and subcategories
4. Installment purchase tracking
5. Recurring expense tracking
6. Monthly budgeting based on customizable rules
7. Month finalization with open and closed status
8. Bulk deletion of expenses within a month
9. Dashboard with financial insights

---

## Success Criteria

The product will be successful if users can:

* understand their monthly financial situation
* control spending categories
* plan future expenses more effectively
* reduce financial uncertainty in their monthly life

---

## Non-Goals (MVP)

The MVP will **not** include:

* banking integrations
* automatic bank transaction imports
* investment portfolio tracking
* advanced financial forecasting
* complex financial accounting

These features may be evaluated in future iterations.
